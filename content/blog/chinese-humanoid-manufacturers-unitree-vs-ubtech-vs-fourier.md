China leads global humanoid robot production, with three manufacturers dominating different market segments: Unitree, UBTech, and Fourier Intelligence. This comparison analyzes their products, strengths, and ideal use cases.

## Quick Verdict

**Best for Research/Development:** Unitree (G1, H1)
**Best for Commercial Service:** UBTech (Walker series)
**Best for Rehabilitation/Medical:** Fourier Intelligence (GR series)

## Company Profiles

### Unitree Robotics
- **Founded:** 2016
- **Focus:** Affordable, developer-friendly platforms
- **Flagship Humanoids:** [G1](/robots/unitree-g1) ($16K), [H1](/robots/unitree-h1) ($90K)
- **Strength:** Price disruption, strong SDK, quadruped expertise

### UBTech
- **Founded:** 2012
- **Focus:** Commercial service and consumer robotics
- **Flagship Humanoids:** [Walker S2](/robots/ubtech-walker-s2), [Walker S1](/robots/ubtech-walker-s1)
- **Strength:** Commercial deployments, manipulation, battery swap

### Fourier Intelligence
- **Founded:** 2015
- **Focus:** Rehabilitation and embodied AI
- **Flagship Humanoids:** GR-1, GR-2
- **Strength:** Medical/rehab expertise, general-purpose AI

## Strategic Positioning

### Unitree: The Disruptor
- **Strategy:** Aggressive pricing, developer ecosystem
- **Target:** Researchers, educators, developers
- **Advantage:** Lowest prices, best SDK, fastest iteration

### UBTech: The Commercial Pioneer
- **Strategy:** End-to-end service solutions
- **Target:** Enterprise, hospitality, retail
- **Advantage:** Proven deployments, manipulation, support

### Fourier: The Specialist
- **Strategy:** Medical/rehabilitation expertise expanding to general purpose
- **Target:** Healthcare, research institutions
- **Advantage:** Domain expertise, clinical validation
