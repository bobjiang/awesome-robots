Unitree has emerged as the developer-friendly robot company:

## Conclusion

For developers and researchers in 2025, **Unitree platforms offer the best open-source experience**:

- **Go2** for learning and budget projects
- **G1** for humanoid research

Choose your platform based on SDK quality and community, not just hardware specs—the software ecosystem often matters more than the robot itself.
