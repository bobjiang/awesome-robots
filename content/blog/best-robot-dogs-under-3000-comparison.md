Four platforms stand out: [Unitree Go2](/robots/unitree-go2), [Deep Robotics Lite3](/robots/deep-robotics-lite3), Xiaomi CyberDog 2, and [Sony Aibo](/robots/sony-aibo-ers-1000). This comparison helps you choose the right entry-level quadruped.

**Best Overall:** [Unitree Go2](/robots/unitree-go2) ($1,600)

## Detailed Analysis

### Unitree Go2: Best Overall Value

The [Go2](/robots/unitree-go2) dominates the entry-level market with unbeatable price-to-performance.

**Strengths:**
- **Lowest price** ($1,600 for Air version)
- **Fastest speed** (3.5 m/s)
- **Best SDK and ROS2 support**
- **Largest community** and resources
- **LiDAR option** on Pro version
- **GPT voice interaction**

**Best For:** Education, hobbyists, researchers, anyone wanting best value

### Deep Robotics Lite3: Best for Developers

The [Lite3](/robots/deep-robotics-lite3) offers professional SDK quality in an affordable package.

**Strengths:**
- **Lightest weight** (12 kg) - easier to handle
- **Professional SDK** with stable APIs
- **Better path to industrial** (X20/X30 upgrade)
- **Quality documentation**

**Best For:** Developers building products, professional research

### Xiaomi CyberDog 2: Best Design

CyberDog 2 offers premium aesthetics and consumer-friendly features.

**Strengths:**
- **Best industrial design** and fit/finish
- **Consumer-friendly** app and interface
- **Voice and vision AI** features
- **Xiaomi ecosystem** integration

**Limitations:** Limited SDK, smaller developer community

**Best For:** Tech enthusiasts, demos, consumer applications

### Sony Aibo: Best Companion

[Aibo](/robots/sony-aibo-ers-1000) is a companion robot, not a development platform.

**Strengths:**
- **Advanced personality AI** - develops over time
- **Emotional companion** features
- **Premium build quality**
- **Living room safe** (2.2 kg)

**Limitations:** No SDK, not for development, highest price per capability

**Best For:** Pet alternative, companion robot, entertainment
