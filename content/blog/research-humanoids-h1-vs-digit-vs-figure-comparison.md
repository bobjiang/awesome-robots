Advanced robotics research requires capable humanoid platforms with strong SDK support and proven reliability. Three platforms target this market: [Unitree H1](/robots/unitree-h1), [Agility Robotics Digit](/robots/agility-digit), and Figure 02. This comparison helps research institutions select the right platform.

## Quick Verdict

**Best Available Now:** [Unitree H1](/robots/unitree-h1) ($90,000-$150,000)
**Most Mature Platform:** Agility Digit ($250,000)
**Most Advanced AI:** Figure 02 (Partner access only)

## Platform Analysis

### Unitree H1: Best Available Option
**Strengths:**
- World-class locomotion (3.3 m/s speed record)
- Highest payload capacity (30 kg)
- Available for purchase globally
- Strong ROS2/Python SDK
- Active community and support

**Best For:** Locomotion research, manipulation, general robotics R&D

### Agility Digit: Most Mature
**Strengths:**
- Longest deployment history
- Proven industrial pilots (Amazon, Ford)
- Purpose-built for logistics
- Strong enterprise support

**Limitations:** Highest price, limited manipulation

**Best For:** Logistics research, industrial automation R&D

### Figure 02: Most Advanced AI
**Strengths:**
- Advanced reasoning (OpenAI partnership)
- Strong manipulation capabilities
- Significant funding and talent

**Limitations:** Not available for purchase, partner access only

**Best For:** Well-funded institutions with partnership potential
