Quadruped robots are increasingly deployed for security applications—perimeter patrol, surveillance, threat detection, and emergency response. Three platforms lead this market: Boston Dynamics Spot, [Ghost Robotics Vision 60](/robots/ghost-robotics-vision-60), and [Unitree B2](/robots/unitree-b2).

## Quick Verdict

**Most Proven:** Boston Dynamics Spot
**Purpose-Built Security:** Ghost Robotics Vision 60
**Best Value:** Unitree B2

## Specification Comparison

| Feature | Boston Dynamics Spot | Ghost Vision 60 | Unitree B2 |
|---------|---------------------|-----------------|------------|
| **Price** | $74,500+ | ~$100,000+ | ~$25,000 |
| **Weight** | 32.5 kg | 51 kg | 60 kg |
| **Payload** | 14 kg | 10 kg | 40 kg |
| **Speed** | 1.6 m/s | 3 m/s | 6 m/s |
| **Runtime** | 90 min | 3+ hours | 4-6 hours |
| **IP Rating** | IP54/65 | IP67 | IP67 |
| **Target** | Multi-purpose | Defense/Security | Multi-purpose |

## Security Capability Analysis

### Boston Dynamics Spot
**Strengths:** Proven deployments, excellent autonomy, comprehensive payloads
**Security Features:** Thermal imaging, 360° cameras, autonomous patrol routes

### Ghost Robotics Vision 60
**Strengths:** Purpose-built for defense, silent operation, rugged construction
**Security Features:** Military-grade sensors, weapon mounting options (controlled)

### Unitree B2
**Strengths:** Best value, highest speed, longest runtime
**Security Features:** Good sensor payload capacity, cost-effective fleet deployment

## Recommendations

| Application | Best Choice | Why |
|-------------|-------------|-----|
| Corporate campus | Spot | Proven, professional image |
| Military/defense | Vision 60 | Purpose-built, specialized |
| Large perimeter | B2 | Speed, runtime, fleet cost |
| Critical infrastructure | Spot | Reliability, support |
| Cost-sensitive | B2 | 1/3 the price of alternatives |

## Conclusion

For most commercial security applications, **Spot offers proven reliability and comprehensive support**. For budget-conscious deployments or large-area coverage, **B2 provides excellent value**. **Vision 60** serves specialized defense and government applications.
