Educational humanoid robots enable hands-on learning in robotics, AI, and programming. Three platforms serve different segments of the education market: [SoftBank NAO](/robots/softbank-nao), [Unitree R1](/robots/unitree-r1), and [UBTech Walker C](/robots/ubtech-walker-c).

## Quick Verdict

**Best for K-12:** SoftBank NAO ($7,500)
**Best Value:** [Unitree R1](/robots/unitree-r1) ($5,900)
**Best for Advanced Programs:** UBTech Walker C ($25,000)

## Specification Comparison

| Feature | SoftBank NAO | Unitree R1 | UBTech Walker C |
|---------|--------------|------------|-----------------|
| **Price** | $7,500 | $5,900 | $25,000 |
| **Height** | 58 cm | 120 cm | 145 cm |
| **Weight** | 5.4 kg | 25 kg | 63 kg |
| **DoF** | 25 | 26 | 36 |
| **Programming** | Choregraphe, Python | Python, ROS2 | Python, Blockly |
| **Target** | K-12, introductory | University, dev | Advanced, demos |
| **Battery** | 90 min | 2 hours | 2 hours |

## Platform Analysis

### SoftBank NAO
**Strengths:** Proven in 10,000+ schools, extensive curriculum, safe size for K-12
**Best For:** Elementary through high school, introductory robotics

### Unitree R1
**Strengths:** Best price, ROS2 support, human-scale, strong SDK
**Best For:** Universities, advanced high schools, budget-conscious programs

### UBTech Walker C
**Strengths:** Full-size manipulation, impressive demos, commercial-grade
**Best For:** Advanced university programs, robotics competitions, showrooms

## Recommendations

| Education Level | Best Choice | Why |
|-----------------|-------------|-----|
| Elementary (K-5) | NAO | Safe size, visual programming |
| Middle School | NAO | Established curriculum |
| High School | R1 or NAO | R1 for advanced, NAO for intro |
| University Intro | R1 | Value, ROS2, scalable |
| University Advanced | Walker C | Full capabilities |
| Research Lab | R1 | SDK, price, community |
